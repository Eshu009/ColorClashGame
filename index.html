Color Clash

Player 1 (Red): 0
AI (Blue): 0

Game Rules

  • Click on a cell to place your color.
  • Special cells apply effects such as doubling regions, reversing colors, blocking areas, or revealing empty cells.
  • Each power-up can only be used a limited number of times.
  • The game ends when no empty cells are left.
  • The player with the most cells in their color wins.

Power-Up Colors

Red: Place your color to claim a cell.
Blue: AI color; place to claim a cell.
Green: Doubles the size of the player's region.
Yellow: Reverses colors of adjacent cells.
Magenta: Blocks an area around the cell.
Cyan: Reveals empty cells around the special cell.
